What is Our Mission?

Welcome to Sound of Jazz, your premier guide to discovering the vibrant jazz scene in the Pacific Northwest. Our mission is to connect jazz enthusiasts with the rich tapestry of live jazz events happening throughout the region, from the bustling cities to the serene coastal towns.
Sound of Jazz is dedicated to being the ultimate resource for jazz lovers seeking live performances, festivals, and jam sessions across the Pacific Northwest. We provide a comprehensive and up-to-date listing of events, ensuring you never miss out on the jazz experiences that matter most to you.
However, who are we to tell you about jazz? We are only jazz loving individuals who only seek to share that passion with our community in the PNW. Our best interest is in making local jazz events accessible and helping others find community within the jazz scene.
Navigating the expansive and diverse jazz scene in the Pacific Northwest can be daunting. Our website simplifies this process, offering a user-friendly platform where you can easily search for events by location, date, and type. Whether you’re looking for a cozy club performance in Seattle or a grand jazz festival in Portland, Sound of Jazz has you covered.

The Importance of Jazz Community

Jazz is more than just music; it's a cultural phenomenon that brings people together. By helping you find live jazz events, we aim to foster a deeper appreciation for this timeless genre and support the artists and venues that keep jazz alive in the Pacific Northwest.
The jazz community in the Pacific Northwest is a thriving, dynamic collective of musicians, fans, and venues. It’s a community that thrives on collaboration, creativity, and shared passion. By promoting jazz events, Sound of Jazz strengthens this community, encouraging connections and collaborations that enrich the cultural fabric of our region.

Exploring and Discovering New Places and Music

One of the joys of jazz is its boundless potential for exploration and discovery. Through Sound of Jazz, we invite you to embark on a journey through the diverse landscapes of the Pacific Northwest, uncovering hidden gems and iconic venues alike. Experience the thrill of discovering new artists and enjoying beloved standards in fresh, exciting ways. Join us at Sound of Jazz and let us guide you through the unparalleled world of jazz in the Pacific Northwest. Your next great jazz adventure awaits!
