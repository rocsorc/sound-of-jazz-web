What is Our Mission?

Welcome to Sound of Jazz! We are here to guide our viewers, whether they are jazz enthusiasts or just getting into it, into discovering the vibrant jazz scene in the Seattle area. Our mission is to connect folk through the rich tapestry of live jazz events happening throughout their local areas in the bustling city that embodies the PNW; There are hidden jazz gems just waiting to be attended. Sound of Jazz is dedicated to being the ultimate resource for jazz lovers seeking live performances, festivals, and jam sessions. We provide comprehensive and up-to-date listings of events, ensuring you will never miss out on the jazz experiences that matter most to you.

Who are we to tell you about jazz?

We are only jazz loving individuals who only seek to share that passion with our communities in the PNW, specifically Seattle. Our best interest is in making local jazz events accessible and assisting people find jazz communities in their areas.

Navigating the expansive and diverse jazz scene in the Pacific Northwest can be arduous. Our website simplifies this process, offering a user-friendly platform where you can easily search for events. Whether you’re looking for a cozy club performance or a grand jazz festival, Sound of Jazz has you covered.

PNW Jazz

What makes our jazz in the Pacific Northwest different from other jazz?

Jazz in the PNW is lively and striking, playing with innovation and influence within jazz. Many different genres and styles of jazz exist due to many different cultures, history, and locations across the globe. Jazz has permeated multiple global music scenes, evolving and cross-pollinating to form electrifying hybrids. Jazz in the PNW supports and participates in this ever-evolving music that actively connects individuals and creates community. There are varieties of intimate jazz cafes, galleries, dedicated night clubs,and grand concert spaces spread among us, just waiting to be discovered.

An amazing example of this that we’d like to shout out is an organization founded and focused on the idea of jazz as an evolving art form called Earshot Jazz. They are based in the Seattle area, active since 1984 and hold concert presentations and strive to cultivate a vivacious community of artists, educators, and audiences.

The Importance of Jazz Community

Jazz is absolutely more than just music; it’s a cultural phenomenon that brings people together, exerts emotion, and is a great art. By helping you find live jazz events, we aim to foster a deeper appreciation for this timeless genre and support the artists and venues that keep jazz alive in the Pacific Northwest. The jazz communities within the PNW are a thriving, dynamic collective of musicians, fans, and venues. It is a community that thrives on collaboration, creativity, and shared passion. By promoting jazz events, Sound of Jazz strengthens this community, encouraging connections and collaborations that enrich the cultural fabric of our region.
